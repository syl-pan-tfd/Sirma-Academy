Washington [US], June 13 (ANI): Who can forget Kate Winslet and Leonardo DiCaprio-starrer 'Titanic', which was released in 1997. The stunning actor recalled the shooting for a romantic 'I'm flying' scene with Leonardo, according to The Hollywood Reporter.

The audience loved the on-screen chemistry between Leonardo's character Jack Dawson and Kate Winslet's charming role of Rose DeWitt Bukater. The two represented completely different classes.

The 'I'm flying' is an iconic scene from the film in which Jack and Rose are standing on the bow of the ship with their hands wide open.

Kate looked back and remembered filming a sequence in which her character Ross kisses DiCaprio's Jack.

"My God, he's quite the romancer, isn't he? No wonder every young girl in the world wanted to be kissed by Leonardo DiCaprio," said Winslet.
